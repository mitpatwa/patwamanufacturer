## Content Distribution Strategy

### On-Site SEO Optimization
- **Internal Linking**: Each article links to 3-5 related articles and 2-3 product pages
- **Image SEO**: All images optimized with descriptive alt text, compressed for speed
- **Schema Markup**: Article, FAQ, and How-To schema on all relevant posts
- **Meta Descriptions**: Compelling, keyword-rich descriptions under 160 characters
- **URL Structure**: Clean, keyword-focused URLs (e.g., /blog/what-is-passementerie)

### Off-Site Promotion
- **Pinterest**: Create 3-5 pins per article with rich pins enabled
- **Instagram**: Share article snippets as carousel posts and stories
- **LinkedIn**: Share trade/B2B focused articles in industry groups
- **Email Newsletter**: Feature 1-2 articles per month to subscriber list
- **Guest Posting**: Repurpose content for design blogs (Apartment Therapy, Houzz)

### Backlink Strategy
- **Industry Citations**: Submit articles to design resource directories
- **Designer Outreach**: Offer to feature designers' work in case studies
- **Press Releases**: Announce major trend articles through PR distribution
- **Influencer Collaboration**: Partner with interior design influencers for co-content
